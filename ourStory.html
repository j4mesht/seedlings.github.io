It All Started With Sending one kid to School

As you read through this story, you will learn that education is a privilege in Haiti. There is no established, free, public education system
so in order to go to school, each student has to come up with the funds each year for tuition, supplies, and a uniform.

1994

Founder Starts School in Haiti

Our founder's parents faced significant challenges to fund his kindergarten education. They had to be resourceful, leveraging various means to gather the necessary funds. This included taking odd job fixing things for people and selling handmade crafts. Their sacrifices and determination underscore the importance of education and serve as the foundation for Seeds Into Roots.

1999

From Haiti to The US

His mother moved from Haiti to the United States and began sending money home for her children to continue with school and to provide a better life for her kids with the plan to move her children to the US

2010

Devastating Earthquake Hits Haiti

In January 2012, a devastating earthquake struck Haiti, leaving a trail of destruction in its wake. This catastrophic event affected millions, flattening entire neighborhoods, destroying schools, and altering countless lives forever. The quake not only demolished infrastructures but also deepened the educational crisis by displacing thousands of school-aged children.

2012

A new beginning

Founder moves to the US with siblings and began the process of learning English and earning his GED.

2021

Founding Movement

Made a deal with God, “If you provide a way, I will send as many kids to school as I can.” A few months later he lands a job and is able to send 15 children in Haiti to school.

2022

Why Not 50?

A friend challenged him to DREAM BIGGER. What if he could help send 50 children to school?

2023

Seeds Into Roots

Seeds Into Roots is established as a 501(3)C Non Profit and is sending 50 children in Haiti to school.

Why does this matter

Without an education, the people of Haiti are left to look to leaders in a fallen government for direction. Seeds Into Roots exists to empower children to grow up in to community leaders who will effect change where they live and help bring up generations of seeds who will be planted into roots for generations to come.
